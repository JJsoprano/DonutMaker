What is Fred’s Bakery?

Our "Fred the Baker's Donut Maker Clicker" game is a nostalgic tribute to the iconic advertising character, Fred the Baker, from **Dunkin' Donuts**. Portrayed by the late actor **Michael Vale**, Fred was famous for his perpetually sleepy, yet dedicated, demeanor and his classic catchphrase: "Time to make the donuts!"

For 15 years (from 1982 to 1997), Fred symbolized Dunkin' Donuts' commitment to baking fresh donuts every morning, long before dawn. His tireless dedication resonated with millions and made him a beloved cultural icon. This game aims to capture a bit of that simple, hardworking charm as you build your own donut empire!
