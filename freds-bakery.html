The Legacy of Fred the Baker

Before dawn, every morning, Fred was at his craft, proclaiming "Time to Make the Donuts!" His dedication is the inspiration behind our very own Donut Maker Clicker.

A Timeless Tradition

The Icon

Fred the Baker was a beloved advertising character for Dunkin' Donuts, famously portrayed by Michael Vale from 1981 to 1997. His commitment to fresh donuts resonated with millions.

The Catchphrase

His iconic line, "Time to make the donuts!", became a pop culture phenomenon, symbolizing dedication, hard work, and the simple joy of a fresh treat.

Our Inspiration

Our game pays homage to Fred's tireless efforts, letting you experience the satisfaction of building your own donut empire, one click at a time.
